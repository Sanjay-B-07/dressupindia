About Us

DressUp India by
Ramachandra

Founded by Ramachandra in Mysore, DressUp India has grown into one of Karnataka's most trusted garment manufacturing companies. With over 17 years of hands-on experience, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver uniforms and apparel that truly represent your brand.

From our state-of-the-art facility in Mysore, we serve businesses across India — from schools and hospitals to corporate offices and hotels. Every stitch reflects our commitment to quality, precision, and timely delivery.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
+
Our minimum order quantity is 50 pieces per design/style. For bulk orders of 500+ pieces, we offer special pricing and priority production scheduling.
How long does manufacturing take?
+
Standard turnaround time is 10-15 business days for orders under 500 pieces. For bulk orders above 500 pieces, production takes 20-30 days. Rush orders can be accommodated — contact us to discuss.
Do you provide fabric samples before production?
+
Yes! We send fabric swatches and a sample garment for approval before starting full production. This ensures you're 100% happy with the material, color, and fit.
Can you add our company logo to uniforms?
+
Absolutely. We offer logo embroidery, screen printing, sublimation printing, and woven patches. Share your logo in high resolution and we'll create a mockup for approval.
Do you deliver outside Karnataka?
+
Yes, we deliver Pan India through reliable logistics partners. We serve clients in Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Delhi NCR, and across all Indian states.
What payment modes are accepted?
+
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI payments, and cheques. Typically 50% advance at order confirmation and 50% before dispatch. EMI options available for large orders.
